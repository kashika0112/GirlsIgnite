Who is a STEMinist?
Someone who advocates for equal representation of social groups in STEM. STEM is a global initiative that is not bound by race, ethnicity, politics, or stereotypes.
What does the current stats say about women in STEM in India?
“Of the 2.8 lakh engineers and scientists employed in research and engineering institutions across the country; only 14 per cent are women, which is 39,000.” Textile Minister Smriti Irani pointed out at the Women Science Congress.
What are the global biases against women working at technical jobs?
1. Prove-it-again bias:76% of women engineers reported having to prove themselves over and over to get the same level of respect as their colleagues.
2. Tightrope bias:77% of women engineers reported that they were confined to a narrower range of acceptable behaviours than their colleagues.
3. Maternal wall bias:40% of women engineers reported bias against mothers at their workplaces.
4. Tug of war bias:45% of women reported that they had to compete with their female colleagues to get the one “woman’s spot” available
